The Building

The property comprises a comprehensively refurbished grade A 1990s office building providing office space over basement to fifth floors. The available accommodation comprises the fifth floor and the fourth floor which is offered with generous provision of floor boxes & new carpet, the space has been recently refurbished throughout, resulting in offices that are bright with generous ceiling heights and abundance of natural light

  Alexander Reece Thompson
Chartered Surveyors

Alexander Reece Thomson LLP are responsible for the management of the day to day running of the building on behalf of the Landlord. They regularly liaise with the occupiers and ensure all queries are dealt with appropriately. Their duties include asset management, rent collection, service charge management and administration, overseeing fire safety and health and safety, as well as managing third party suppliers.

Constructed of brick and stone external walls, timber and concrete floors, metal framed windows and doors, with the roof being flat, the property provides a prominent headquarters office building arranged over basement, lower ground, ground and five upper floors.
