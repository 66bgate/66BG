Location

66 Buckingham Gate is on the north side of Buckingham Gate being one of London's most prestigious addresses, at its junction with Caxton Street and Vandon Street is a short walk from both St James’s Park underground (District and Circle lines) and Victoria Station (District, Circle and Victoria underground & mainlines). The building is situated directly opposite the St James Court Hotel and the Bank and Bistro restaurant, and adjacent to the new development Buckingham Green, the property is a minutes’ walk from Victoria Street and Cardinal Place and the many leisure and retail facilities there.
